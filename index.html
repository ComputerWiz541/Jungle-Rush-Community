At JRC we make incredibly realistic games based on real life events, so far we have only made one game based on the Vietnam War. That game is called Jungle Rush, we started making Jungle Rush on August 3rd 2023, back when it was just a one-man team. Jungle Rush is an ultra realistic Vietnam War simulator. Jungle Rush features two teams, the Vietcong and the South Vietnamese, amazing graphics, high terrain detail, realistic gun models and animations featuring the iconic MP5 and AK-74, and more, pushing Roblox Studio to its limits. The iconic JRC logo is based on a real-life combat medal, with realistic colors.
